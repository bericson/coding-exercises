Exercise One

Convert Number Amount to Words

Guidelines on Amount Format:
  • Enter only numbers, and if the amount includes decimals, only one decimal point ('.')
  • Amounts with decimals entered beyond two places will be rounded to the second decimal place (i.e., hundredths)
  • Any commas entered will be ignored (including use of the comma to indicate the decimal point, e.g. European notation)
  • Any letters entered will invalidate the input and will not be accepted (including exponent notation, e.g., '+e10')
  • Converter does not support numbers/amounts beyond the millions range (i.e., greater than 999,999,999.999)
  • The amount input field is intended merely as a mechanism for easily entering a valid amount to test the functionality of the converter, and can likely be intentionally broken if one is trying to break it (e.g., pasting illegal values, putting multiple decimals, etc.)
