Exercise One

Convert Number Amount to Word Form

Notes on Amount Format:
  • Enter only numbers, and if the amount includes decimals, only one decimal point ('.')
  • Amounts with decimals entered beyond two places will be rounded to the second decimal place (hundredths)
  • Any commas entered will be ignored (including use of the comma to indicate the decimal point, e.g. European notation)
  • Any letters entered will invalidate the input and will not be accepted (including exponent notation, e.g., '+e10')
  • Negative amounts entered will simply be interpreted as the positive value of the amount
Converted Result: One thousand four hundred eighty two and 45/100
